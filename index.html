Portfolio

Created for artist Katie Lee, Convent was an online map designed to accompany an exhibition set across the grounds of Abbotsford Convent. Presenting viewers with a map designed in collaboration with the artist, the location of the orange points could be easily set and added to by the artist without modifying the website code.

Working as part of a team, I developed the back-end for the latest iteration of Duncan's Roulette Free-for-All app, which he uses to to create popular videos on a daily basis. This new version enabled him to give accounts or one-time-use codes to viewers or other content creators, opening up a wide range of new opportunities.

Created for the Pokémon video game community, pokepast.es is a custom pastebin for players to build, share and discuss teams. Utilising an existing text format, PokePaste generates coloured highlighting and images for any team, and creates a unique link that can be shared indefinitely. The website has received over 700,000 submissions.
